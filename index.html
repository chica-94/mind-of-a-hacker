The Mind of a Hacker

Think outside the box

Adopt a hacker mindset to improve threat detection

For the aspiring security professionals and everyday tech user that will keep adversaries from gaining access to their device. They typically use a 5-step methodology from discovery to exploitation. This mindset emphasizes exploitation problem-solving abilities, creativity, and constant learning.

Get to know techniques and tools they use

Phase 1 - Reconnaissance/Footprinting

Initial phase of a systematic data-gathering process

Methods used:

  • Open Source Intelligence
  • Active and Passive
  • Information Gathering

Tools used:

  • Angry IP Scanner
  • Traceroute
  • Recon-ng
  • TheHarvester

This entails getting as much information as you can about the target without interacting directly with the target. The greatest resource for recon is the Internet, and the greatest tool is the search engine, Google.
